You've got the point, who needs it?

At the time, when P/E ratio of over 100 does not scare anyone because the expected revenue growth rates seem to double every other year one needs a solid and grounded foundation when it comes to investing. Investors no longer deem it unprudent to see Nasdaq exceeding before-pandemic highs. That is when value investing comes in handy in order to check in with real cashflows and interest rates.

How it works

We rely on income-based approach

We believe that one of the main misconceptions of our era is undervaluing the importance of generating cashflows. That is why we suggest using income-based approach that can provide a clearer vision of company's value.

  • Assessment is made by means of DCF model.
  • We provide you with not just one estimation, but with a range of values.
  • Modelling of worst-case, base-case and best-case scenarios is achieved through manipulating discount rate and post-terminal growth rate.

Disclaimer

Income-based approach should be used as an component of complete business valuation process due to the fact that company's value cannot be fully described by means of only one instrument.
